Indrashil University

Indrashil University was established with the vision of delivering high-quality education, innovation, and industry-focused learning.

The university offers programs in engineering, management, science, pharmacy, and emerging technologies.

It is equipped with modern laboratories, digital classrooms, research centers, innovation cells, sports facilities, and a central library.

The university focuses on academic excellence, practical learning, entrepreneurship, and student development.

Indrashil University aims to prepare students for global careers through knowledge, discipline, and innovation.
